# Sponsors

ts-analytics is open source and free to use. Sponsorships help us dedicate more time to development and maintenance.

## Current Sponsors

We would like to extend our thanks to the following sponsors for funding Stacks development:

- [JetBrains](https://www.jetbrains.com/)
- [The Solana Foundation](https://solana.com/)

## Become a Sponsor

If you or your company benefits from ts-analytics, please consider sponsoring its development.

### GitHub Sponsors

Support the project through [GitHub Sponsors](https://github.com/sponsors/chrisbbreuer).

### Benefits

Sponsors receive:
- Priority support
- Logo placement on documentation
- Early access to new features
- Direct communication channel

## Why Sponsor?

- **Sustainability**: Help ensure long-term maintenance
- **Features**: Fund development of features you need
- **Community**: Support the open source ecosystem
- **Recognition**: Show your commitment to open source
