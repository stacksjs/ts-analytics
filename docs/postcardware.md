# Postcardware

Stacks OSS will always stay open-sourced, and we will always love to receive postcards from wherever Stacks is used!

## Why Postcardware?

We love connecting with developers around the world. A postcard is a fun, tangible way to:

- Share where you're building from
- Tell us about your project
- Brighten our day!

## What to Include

- Your name or company
- Where you're writing from
- What you're building with ts-analytics
- A fun local fact or recommendation

## Received Postcards

We'll share postcards we receive (with permission) on our social media and here on this page.
